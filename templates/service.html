What we provide

We provide different online sessions for people who feel they can't express their thoughts. They can start off with alone time with a counselor then once they have built up the corage to talk with more people, we can offer group sessions.

Why this page is set up like this

You can't have a set price for helping people with their emotions until you have a set amount of time you want to have the lesson for. Because you can do sessions alone, with the parents, or a group, the prices will always be changed.
